Our Mission

South House Public Relations is a project-based Public Relations company specializing in food & beverage, hospitality, retail and real estate.

We leverage our close relationships with journalists to provide game-changing results that put your company at the forefront of its industry.

Our goal is to get your business the most organic exposure possible. We incorporate modern marketing with word-of-mouth marketing tactics to get people talking.
